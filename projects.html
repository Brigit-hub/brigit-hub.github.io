Challenge

The organization needed clarity on profitability across diverse product lines and market segments. While metrics existed, the reporting structure lacked coherence, making it difficult for stakeholders to quickly identify performance trends or strategic opportunities.

Solution

Developed a structured analytical framework by cleaning 700 rows of transaction data, standardizing key fields, and designing a dashboard presentation that presents revenue, costs, profit, and margin metrics in an intuitive, executive-ready format.
